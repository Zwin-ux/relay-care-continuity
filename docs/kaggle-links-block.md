## Short Project Summary

RELAY Care Continuity is a local-first reviewer workspace for wildfire evacuation shelters. It uses Gemma 4 replay outputs and local Ollama verification to group messy source reports into a care-continuity ledger, hold unsafe health claims for review, show missing required information, and keep handoff unavailable until the review state is complete.

## Safety Boundary

RELAY does not contact emergency services, dispatch responders, provide medical advice, verify live conditions, or replace official systems. The public demo uses mock wildfire-replay data.
